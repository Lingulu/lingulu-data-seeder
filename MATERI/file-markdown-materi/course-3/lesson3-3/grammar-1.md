# Passive Voice Structure

## Definition

**Passive Voice** digunakan ketika kita ingin menekankan **objek (penerima aksi)** atau **aksi itu sendiri**, daripada **subjek (pelaku)**.

## Basic Pattern

Pola dasar passive voice **selalu**:
**To Be + Verb 3 (Past Participle)**

## Tense Changes

### Present Passive

- The room is cleaned every day.

### Past Passive

- The room was cleaned yesterday.

### Future Passive

- The room will be cleaned tomorrow.
